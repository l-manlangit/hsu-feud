Why Does FEU Have Two Clinics?

FEU Diliman has two clinics to ensure that health services are accessible to everyone across its campus. This setup allows students, faculty, and staff to receive timely medical attention regardless of their location within the university.

The Importance of Health

Health is the foundation of everything we do. By prioritizing health, FEU ensures that its community can thrive academically, professionally, and personally. The presence of two clinics underscores the university's commitment to the well-being of its members.

Frequently Asked Questions

The clinic provides basic check-ups, first aid, emergency response, and health consultations.

The clinic follows strict hygiene protocols, ensures proper sanitation, and adheres to health guidelines to maintain a safe environment.

In case of a medical emergency, students should immediately inform the nearest faculty or staff member and proceed to the clinic for assistance.

The clinic assesses the student's condition, provides necessary treatment, and contacts guardians if further medical attention is required.

Students should know how to clean and bandage minor wounds, apply ice to reduce swelling, and stay calm during emergencies.
